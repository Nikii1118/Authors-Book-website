Udham Singh

A National hero was born on 26 December, 1899 at Sunam (District Sangrur). He killed Michael O’Dwyer and wounded three English officers in London. In Court, during trial proceeding in front of Judge, he said, (shouting) "I do not care about sentence of death. It means nothing at all. I do not care about dying or anything. We are suffering from the British Empire. I am not afraid to die. I am proud to die". Udham Singh was hanged in London on 31 July, 1940 at the age of nearly 40. When judge made the proclamation of his sentence of death. He shouted three words Inqlab, Inqlab, Inqlab and down with British Imperialism..

I have seen the world. The only ambition have left, I like to see India free."

-Udham Singh

Railway Land Management

Railway land is the biggest asset of Indians Railways from where it can earn crores of rupees monthly by proper commercial utilization of it. The total area of land under Railway Network is 45,8589 hectares out of which approximately 46,409 hectares is vacant as on 31.03.2014. The vacant land which is not required by railways for its immediate operational needs to be utilized for commercial development. These books are specially written for ground level staff of all departments of railway, facing practical problems in day-today working while dealing with railway land issues. An attempt has been made to make this book upto date.

"Railway may earn crores from its land by management."

-Rakesh Kumar
